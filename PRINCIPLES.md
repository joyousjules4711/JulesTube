# 💚 JulesTube Principles

JulesTube is more than a music player.

It is a shared living room experience.

---

## 1. Music comes first

Software should never distract from the music.

Every feature should make listening easier, warmer or more fun.

---

## 2. The couch is the center

JulesTube is built for people sitting together.

Not for dashboards.
Not for metrics.
Not for endless menus.

For the couch.

---

## 3. Technology should disappear

The best interface is the one people stop noticing.

JulesTube should feel simple, calm and natural.

---

## 4. Everyone gets a turn

JulesTube is a shared queue.

Every guest can become the DJ.

---

## 5. Build with joy

JulesTube should stay playful, personal and human.

No dark patterns.
No clutter.
No unnecessary complexity.

Just music, people and good evenings.

---

> Turn strangers into guests.  
> Turn guests into DJs.
